Madeleine Chang

Space Policy Dev | Satellite Engineer

I am the Director of Policy at the Satellite Industry Association (SIA), a nonprofit trade group building consensus within the commercial satellite industry and working directly with the U.S. government on space policy issues.

Previously, I was a visiting fellow on the Aerospace Security Project at the Center for Strategic and International Studies (CSIS) and a software engineer at Lockheed Martin within the National Security Space division.

I hope to help set positive norms in space activity so our future in space will be safe, peaceful, and beneficial to all.

You can reach me at mchang[at]sia.org.
